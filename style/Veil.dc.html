FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do my photos get uploaded anywhere?+

No. Everything runs locally in your browser — your photos never leave your device, on Free or Pro.

What exactly does XenLens remove?+

GPS coordinates, camera make & model, the capture date & time, and software / OS tags embedded in the file.

Will cleaning change how my photo looks?+

No. The pixels stay the same — XenLens just re-saves the image without the hidden data attached.

Which file types are supported?+

JPEG and PNG today, with more formats on the way.

Is my original file modified?+

Never. You download a clean copy; your original stays exactly as it was.
